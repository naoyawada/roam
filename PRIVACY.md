# Privacy Policy

**Roam — Location Log**
Last updated: March 24, 2026

## Overview

Roam is a personal location logging app that records which city you are in each night to build a travel history over time. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how Roam handles your data.

## Data Collection

### Location Data
Roam accesses your device's location in the background to determine which city you are in. When a location fix is obtained, the coordinates are sent to Apple's servers for reverse geocoding (to convert coordinates into a city name). The developer does not receive or store your raw coordinates — they are used only in-transit to resolve a city name on your device.

Raw location data is never stored by Roam.

### Personal Data
Roam does not require you to create an account or sign in. The app does not collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.

## Data Storage

Your city-level travel history and city statistics are stored on your device and sync to your private iCloud account via CloudKit. This data is only accessible to you — the developer has no access to your iCloud data.

Raw location coordinates are never stored by the app.

## iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud, Roam syncs your daily entries and city records to your private iCloud container. This allows your data to be available across your devices. Apple's standard iCloud privacy and security policies apply to this data.

## Data Sharing

Roam does not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external data flow is:

- **Apple (reverse geocoding):** Coordinates are sent to Apple to resolve a city name. Apple's privacy policy governs this request.
- **Apple (iCloud):** Your travel history syncs to your private iCloud account under Apple's data practices.

No data is shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or any other third parties.

## Your Control

You can delete your data at any time from within the app or by deleting the app from your device. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device's Settings, which will prevent Roam from logging new city entries. You can disable iCloud sync for Roam in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

## Children's Privacy

Roam is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
